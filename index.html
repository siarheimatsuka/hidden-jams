Why Today’s musicRankings Don’t Work
Great songs get buried by the algorithm
Streaming data favors hype, not the songs fans keep coming back to.
New listeners miss what truly hits
Most people try the top song first, but it rarely shows the artist at their best.
Artists can’t see what really lands
Without real fan input, artists guess which songs actually resonate most.
We’re Making Song Discovery Fairer and
Fan-Driven
Rather than relying on the commercial popularity or the latest releases, we put the power in the hands of the fans. Every fans can rank their favorite songs, and through the wisdom of the crowd, we’re able to curate a given artist’s fan favorite song rankings from top to bottom.
Voted by real fans
Every ranking is based on listener submissions, not stream counts
Hidden gems rise to the top
Even low-play songs can rank high if fans believe they’re worth hearing.
Honest, per-artist rankings
Each artist gets their own song list. No cross-genre or leaderboard bias.

We believe that true music discovery should be shaped by among listeners. That’s why we operate as a non-profit to keep our platform fan-first and unbiased.
Why We’re Non-Profit
We want to build a transparent and community-driven ranking system without the influence of commercial interests or industry pressure.
How It Benefits Fans and Artists
Artists can see what truly resonates without worrying about commercial biases. Fans get a clear, unfiltered view of what the community really loves.
We used The Hidden Jams to drive fan engagement in advance of our 40th anniversary shows this spring. It was amazing, fans loved it, and we even modified our set lists based on their responses.
